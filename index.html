What We Offer

Complete IT Solutions for Every Need

From hardware repairs to network infrastructure, we deliver comprehensive technology services tailored to your requirements.

Computer Repair, Hardware & Software

Desktop diagnostics, hardware repair, virus/malware removal, OS reinstallation, blue screen and boot failure fixes, data recovery from failed drives and corrupted storage, and RAM/SSD/GPU upgrades. Most machines see a 2–3× speed boost with an SSD swap alone. No fix, no charge on diagnostics.

Laptop Repair

Screen replacement, keyboard and trackpad repair, battery and charging port replacement, hinge and casing repair, and motherboard-level diagnostics and soldering for all major brands — Lenovo, HP, Dell, Asus, Acer, Apple, and more.

Network Setup, Configuration & Peripheral Devices

End-to-end network and workstation setup for homes, offices, and internet cafés. Covers router/switch config, WiFi optimization, VLAN segmentation, MikroTik hotspot and billing systems, Cat5e/Cat6 structured cabling and patch panels, desktop and laptop deployment, POS system setup, printers, and peripherals. Also includes biometric time and attendance systems — fingerprint, RFID, and face recognition — with full software integration and user enrollment.

CCTV Installation

IP and analog camera installation for homes, offices, and commercial sites — night vision, remote mobile viewing via app, motion detection, and flexible system sizes (4, 8, and 16-channel packages available). Also installs fire alarm systems (smoke detectors, heat sensors, alarm bells, and control panels). Includes DVR/NVR setup, cable runs, and mobile app configuration.

Printer Repair & Maintenance

Inkjet and laser printer repair and preventive maintenance — paper jam clearing, print head cleaning, roller and pickup pad replacement, waste ink pad reset, driver installation, and wired/wireless network printer setup and sharing configuration.

Windows & Software Installation

Fresh Windows installation (10/11), full driver setup, genuine license activation, antivirus configuration, and software deployment — Microsoft 365, Adobe Creative Suite, accounting tools, POS software, and any business-specific application your workflow requires.

IT Consulting & Systems Administration

Technology advisory for growing businesses — network and server infrastructure planning, hardware/software procurement, Active Directory and Microsoft 365 setup and management, vendor evaluation, and on-call systems administration. Backed by hands-on enterprise and government IT experience.

Livestreaming & Event AV Setup

Full-production livestreaming for sports tournaments, festivals, corporate events, and church services — OBS Studio setup, multi-camera switching, graphics overlay and scoreboard integration, audio mixing, and real-time on-site troubleshooting to keep your stream running without interruption.
